Freezing and cooling

Storage refrigerators are cold rooms that are used to preserve goods, food, and products that need to stay at a low temperature. These rooms are lined with certain materials to prevent leakage through walls and doors, to keep cooled products from spoiling for as long as possible. We can say that storage refrigerators are the largest alternative to home refrigerators, and are usually used in food and dairy products factories, in large food stores, or in hospitals and some clinics and medical centers
