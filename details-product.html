Product information

description

An effective insect growth regulator with a low concentration in the form of an emulsifier concentrate (EC) that works to control whiteflies of all kinds by preventing the hatching of eggs and disturbing the growth and development of nymphs, which leads to their death. It does not affect the insect's nervous system, and it is specialized in certain stages in the insect's life and not others, and thus it is safe for the environment and vital organs.
